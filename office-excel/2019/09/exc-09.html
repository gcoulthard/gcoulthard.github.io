Excel 09: Multiple-Sheet Workbooks

In this module, you learn to work efficiently with large worksheets in a workbook and to prepare a workbook for other users. To this end, you learn to manipulate and format sheet tabs and to protect a worksheet from unwanted modification. You also learn how to insert cell comments to leave notes for other users and how to restrict their data entry to acceptable values to ensure data integrity.

To follow along with the video lessons in this module, make sure
to download and unzip the Excel 09 data files to your computer.

01. Consolidating Your Data

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Group worksheets for formatting and printing

  • Consolidate a group of worksheets within a workbook

  • Construct a 3D SUM function to consolidate a workbook

  • Use the Data Consolidate command in a multiple-sheet workbook

Consolidating Data from Multiple Sheets (10:05m) — view on YouTube.

02. Verifying Worksheet Data

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Perform error-checking and solve circular references

  • Add, remove, format, and view cell comments

  • Trace precedents and dependents to analyze formulas

  • Create data validation rules for entering data

  • Protect a cell, worksheet, and workbook

Reviewing and Validating Worksheet Data (16:56m) — view on YouTube.
