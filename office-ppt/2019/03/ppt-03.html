PowerPoint 03: Using Media, Tables, & Charts

This module is where the fun truly begins. You learn to insert, format, and manipulate photos, clip art images, and SmartArt graphics. You also learn to insert and control audio and video clips. Used respectfully, these visual elements can help you engage your audience and to communicate your message more clearly and succinctly. When overused, the effect can be confusing for an audience. By the end of this module, you will have the skills necessary to incorporate multimedia intelligently.

To follow along with the video lessons in this module, make sure
to download and unzip the PowerPoint 03 data files to your computer.

01. Adding Audio & Video

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Insert clip art pictures from your computer and from online searches

  • Size, position, align, and arrange clip art and other objects

  • Insert audio from your computer and from online searches

  • Insert video from your computer and from online searches

  • Modify the play and appearance options for audio and video

Adding Audio and Video Clips (12:03m) — view on YouTube.

02. Adding SmartArt Graphics

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Insert a SmartArt graphic and enter text

  • Format a SmartArt graphic using style and color options

  • Size and move a SmartArt graphic on a slide

Adding SmartArt Graphics (5:28m) — view on YouTube.

03. Adding Tables & Charts

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Create a table structure and enter data

  • Format a table using style and color options

  • Insert an Excel worksheet object for creating a table

  • Insert and format a chart for displaying values graphically

Adding Tables and Charts (7:53m) — view on YouTube.

04. Creating a Photo Album

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Select a Photo Album template for inserting and displaying photos

  • Use the Photo Album command to create a slide show automatically

Creating a Photo Album (4:14m) — view on YouTube.
